About Market Diner

Where classic comfort meets fresh flavors

Since our founding, Market Diner has been a cornerstone of the Seattle dining scene. We're a family-owned establishment that believes in the power of fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes to bring people together.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen is prepared with care, using the finest local ingredients we can source. From our hearty breakfast classics to our fresh seafood specialties, we're committed to serving food that feels like home.

Whether you're grabbing a quick coffee or settling in for a leisurely meal with family, we strive to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere where memories are made over great food.
