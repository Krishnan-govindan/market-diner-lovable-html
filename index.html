About Market Diner

For over two decades, Market Diner has been a beloved fixture in Seattle's iconic Pike Place Market, serving up classic American comfort food with a commitment to fresh, quality ingredients.

Our family-owned diner takes pride in preparing everything to order, from our famous eggs benedict with fresh crab to our hearty burgers made with premium sirloin. We believe great food brings people together, and every dish is crafted with care to create memorable dining experiences.

Whether you're starting your day with our signature breakfast or enjoying a satisfying lunch after exploring the market, we're here to serve you authentic flavors that have made us a local favorite and visitor destination.
